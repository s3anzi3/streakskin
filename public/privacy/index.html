Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

EBK ("Elite Ball Knowledge", "we", "us") is a free sports trivia arcade at eliteballknowledge.web.app. This policy explains what information we handle and why. Short version: we collect the minimum needed to run accounts and leaderboards, we don't sell anything, and we don't run ads or third-party trackers.

What we collect

  • Account info. If you create an account: your email address, a display name you choose, and a password (stored and secured by Firebase Authentication — we never see your password).
  • Gameplay data. Scores, best runs, and play counts per game, tied to your account so leaderboards and your dashboard work.
  • Reports. If you report a display name, we store the report, the name reported, and your account id so we can review it.
  • Local storage. Your best scores are also kept in your browser's localStorage so the games work without an account. This data stays on your device.

What we don't collect

No advertising identifiers, no third-party analytics, no selling or sharing of personal data with marketers, no precise location. We don't knowingly collect personal information from children under 13; if you believe a child has created an account, contact us and we will delete it.

What's public

Your display name and your scores appear on public leaderboards. Pick a display name you're comfortable showing publicly — your email address is never shown to other players.

Who processes your data

EBK runs on Google Firebase (Hosting, Authentication, and Cloud Firestore). Google processes data on our behalf under the Firebase privacy terms. Player photos and team logos are loaded from the leagues' and ESPN's public image servers; those requests are subject to those sites' own policies.

Your choices

  • You can play every game without an account.
  • You can request deletion of your account and its data at any time (see Contact below) and we'll remove it.
  • You can clear your browser's localStorage to remove on-device scores.

Security

Access to account data is restricted by Firebase security rules (you can only write your own data), traffic is served over HTTPS, and score writes are validated and rate-limited. No system is perfectly secure, but we keep the attack surface small: we never store your password and we hold no payment information.

Changes

If this policy changes materially, we'll update this page and the date above.

Contact

Questions, or want your data deleted? Open an issue at github.com/s3anzi3/ebk and we'll take care of it.
